    Past editions

    In this page you can find the list of past editions of the Bertinoro International Spring School from 2011 to 2022 with the associated list of offered courses. The editions are listed in chronological order, from the most recent to the oldest. The editions for 2020 and 2021 were cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
